
# VRC Haptics

The current core of the haptics ecosystem, these are DIY devices around the difficulty of SimeVR trackers to built and share alot of the same tools. 

## Bill Of Materials
 - Micro controller (brains): [ESP32, ESP32-S3, ESP32-C3, ESP8266]
 - MOSFET (Amplify Small signals from micro controller): [ULN2803A]
 - Coin Vibration Motors: [DC_10mmx3mm_3V]
